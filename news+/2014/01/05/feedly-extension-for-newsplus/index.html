Feedly Extension for News+

05 Jan 2014

We proudly announce that Feedly extension is available for News+. Feedly Cloud is one of the most popular feed aggregator on the internet.  You will find the extension on Play Store.

How to use?

  • Install News+ and Feedly extension on your Android device.
  • Open News+ and press on the drop-down in action bar.
  • Select Feedly extension in the list.
  • On Login screen press Login button.
  • Sign into Feedly with your Google Account.
